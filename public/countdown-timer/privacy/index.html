Countdown Timer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Countdown Timer is a simple offline countdown app. This policy explains what data it collects — which is none.

Data collection

Countdown Timer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or device identifiers. The app:

  • Does not require internet access and makes no network requests.
  • Does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs of any kind.
  • Does not request access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, storage, or any other device permission.
  • Does not create accounts, and has no login.

On-device features

The app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to announce countdown cues (e.g. "Rider Ready") and its built-in tone generator for beep sounds. Both run entirely on-device through the Android operating system; no audio or data leaves your device.

Children's privacy

Since the app collects no data whatsoever, it does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this app's data practices ever change, this page will be updated to reflect that.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the app's GitHub repository.
